Flip to Mirror Image

Tap to choose an image or video and tap to open the Rotate menu, where flips the clip to its mirror image horizontally and flips the clip to create a vertical mirror image of the original.


Rotate

Tap to choose an image or video and tap to open the Rotate menu. Tapping rotates the clip counterclockwise by 90 degrees whereas rotates the clip clockwise by 90 degrees.
