Crop an Image

Tap the image to be cropped and tap to open the cropping menu. Adjust the size and angle of both the starting and ending cropped views of the image. When played, the clip will smoothly move from cropped start to the cropped end of the image.



Crop a Video Clip

Tap the video clip to be cropped and tap to open the cropping menu. Adjust the size and angle of both the starting and ending frames of the clip, and the clip will smoothly move from the starting cropped frame to the final cropped frame.



Equal

When cropping, tap to make the start and end positions the same. Whichever position (start or end) is selected will also be set for the other position.
